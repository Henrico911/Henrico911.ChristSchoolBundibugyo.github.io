Our Programmes & Services

From O-Level to A-Level, boarding life to spiritual formation — discover everything CSB has to offer.

What We Offer

A Complete Education for the Whole Person

At Christ School Bundibugyo, education goes beyond the classroom. We invest in every student academically, spiritually, physically, and socially — preparing them to thrive at university and beyond.

01

O-Level Programme (S1–S4)

Our four-year Ordinary Level programme follows the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) curriculum, offering a broad and rigorous education in sciences, humanities, mathematics, and languages. Students sit for Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations at the end of Senior 4.

Mathematics Sciences English History Geography CRE
02

A-Level Science Track (S5–S6)

Our Advanced Level programme is intentionally designed around Mathematics and Sciences to address the critical shortage of medical and engineering professionals in Bundibugyo. Successful completion opens doors to Uganda's top universities. Students sit for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE).

Biology Chemistry Physics Mathematics Computer Studies
03

Full Boarding Experience

Students live on campus throughout each academic term, sleeping in dormitories and eating three meals a day. This arrangement ensures focused study time, structured daily routines, and a tight-knit campus community. Teachers also reside on campus, fostering a family-like atmosphere.

Dormitories 3 Meals Daily Supervised Study Medical Care
04

Spiritual Development & Chapel

Faith is woven into every aspect of CSB life. We hold daily chapel services, weekly Sunday church worship, regular Bible study groups, and student-led prayer meetings. Our goal is to develop students who are not just academically capable, but spiritually grounded and morally courageous.

Daily Chapel Sunday Church Bible Study Prayer Groups
05

Sports & Extracurricular Clubs

A healthy body complements a sharp mind. CSB offers football, netball, volleyball, and athletics. Beyond sports, students engage in debate clubs, environmental clubs, drama, music, and the Christian Union — all developing leadership, teamwork, and character.

Football Netball Debate Drama Music Christian Union
06

Professional Teacher Development

CSB invests heavily in its teachers. Through ongoing professional development workshops, curriculum training, and leadership coaching, we ensure our staff are equipped with the latest pedagogical techniques and remain motivated and spiritually healthy in their vocation.

Pedagogy Training Leadership Curriculum Dev. Mentorship
