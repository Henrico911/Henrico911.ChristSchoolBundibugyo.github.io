Who We Are

A School Rooted in Faith, Growing in Excellence

Nestled in the mountains of western Uganda, Christ School Bundibugyo serves students from Senior 1 through Senior 6. We offer a full boarding experience where students grow academically, spiritually, and personally in a nurturing Christian environment.

Our campus buzzes with chapel services, sports, clubs, and a community of dedicated teachers — many of whom are proud CSB alumni — who live and work alongside students every day.
